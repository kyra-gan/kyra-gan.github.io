About Me

I am currently a postdoctoral fellow in the department of Harvard Statistics, working with Susan Murphy on statistical reinforcement learning in healthcare. I am joining Cornell Tech as an Assistant Professor in ORIE starting in July 2023! I obtained my Ph.D. degree in Operations Research from Tepper School of Business, Carnegie Mellon Univeristy in May 2022. Prior to CMU, I received my BA degrees in Mathematics (with the Ann Kirsten Pokora Prize) and Economics from Smith College in May, 2017.

Research Interests

My current research interests lie in adaptive trial design in digital health (including micro-randomized trials and N-of-1 trials), targeted learning, robust causal discovery in medical data, and fairness in organ transplants. More broadly, I am interested in developing ready for production AI tools in healthcare. A copy of my CV can be found here.
